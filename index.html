SONIC CLASH

Sonic Clash is a 2d sidescroller about Sonic The Hedgehog that is a mixture between speed and platforming. The story is that Dr. Robotnik tricks sonic into falling through a tube which leads him to a christmas green hill zone. There are over 10 playable characters in game, including Mario!
